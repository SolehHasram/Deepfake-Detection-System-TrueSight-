About TrueSight

Welcome to TrueSight, a premier deepfake detection platform crafted to empower individuals and organizations against malicious digital manipulation. In today's digital era where generative AI and face-swapping capabilities are increasingly weaponized, verifying media authenticity has never been more critical. TrueSight answers this challenge through pioneering, multi-layered algorithmic verification.

Our Technical Edge

  • Multi-Model Ensemble Engine: A comprehensive scanner analyzing media integrity, concluding with an aggregated Master Confidence Score.
  • Error Level Analysis (ELA) Core: Deep digital forensic dissection that highlights visually imperceptible pixel tampering and mismatched compression artifacts.
  • "TrueSight AI" Research Assistant: A built-in AI research assistant powered by GPT-4o that fetches the latest deepfake-related news from Google News and provides accurate, up-to-date answers with real article links for reference.

TrueSight goes beyond simply labeling a video "fake or real." We break down the technical justifications—providing you with clear, color-coded interactive dashboards so you understand why the media was flagged. From audio mismatches to unnatural digital artifact blurs, our goal is to restore confidence in global media authenticity.

Truth is not just seen. It is verified.
